      <ol>
        <li>Spread almonds in a large skillet; toast over medium heat until starting to brown, 3 to 5 minutes. Pour almonds into a bowl.</li>
        <li>Melt chocolate in top of a double boiler over simmering water, stirring frequently and scraping down the sides to avoid scorching, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat; pour in almonds and stir until evenly coated.</li>
        <li>Drop spoonfuls of chocolate-almond mixture onto a plate lined with waxed paper.</li>
        <li>Chill until set, about 10 minutes.</li>
