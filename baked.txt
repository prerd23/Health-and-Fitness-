    <h1>Baked Apple with Cinnamon</h1>
  </header>

  <main>
    <section class="recipe">
      <h2>Ingredients</h2>
      
        <li>3 Golden Delicious apples - peeled, cored, and thinly sliced</li>
        <li>⅔ cup water</li>
        <li>2 teaspoons cold butter, cut into small pieces</li>
        <li>1 ½ teaspoons all-purpose flour</li>
        <li>1 teaspoon cornstarch</li>
        <li>½ cup brown sugar</li>
        <li>¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon</li>
        <li>1 pinch salt</li>
      </ul>

      <h2>Instructions</h2>
      <ol>
        <li>Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).</li>
        <li>Layer apple slices on the bottom of an 8-inch glass baking dish.</li>
        <li>Mix water, butter, flour, and cornstarch together in a bowl until no lumps remain. Stir in brown sugar, cinnamon, and salt until smooth. Pour apples into the baking dish.</li>
        <li>Bake in the preheated oven, stirring every 10 to 15 minutes, until soft and juicy, about 45 minutes. </li>
    </section>
