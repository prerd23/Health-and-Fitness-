      <h2>Ingredients</h2>
        <h3>Sauce:</h3>
      
        <li>⅔ cup fresh orange juice</li>
        <li>⅓ cup fresh lemon juice</li>
        <li>⅓ cup packed brown sugar</li>
        <li>½ teaspoon grated orange zest</li>
        <li>½ teaspoon grated lemon zest</li>
        <li>½ cup brown sugar</li>
        <li>1 teaspoon vanilla extract</li>

        <h3>Salad</h3>
        <li>2 cups cubed fresh pineapple</li>
        <li>2 cups strawberries, hulled and sliced</li>
        <li> kiwi fruit, peeled and sliced</li>
        <li>3 bananas, sliced</li>
        <li>2 oranges, peeled and sectioned</li>
         <li>1 cup seedless grapes</li>
        <li>2 cups blueberries</li>
       
      </ul>

      <h2>Instructions</h2>
      <ol>
        <h3>For the Sauce:</h3>
        <li>Bring orange juice, lemon juice, brown sugar, orange zest, and lemon zest to a boil in a saucepan over medium-high heat. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until slightly thickened, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla extract. Set aside to cool.

        </li>
        <h3>For the Salad:</h3>
        <li>Layer fruit in a large, clear glass bowl in this order: pineapple, strawberries, kiwi fruit, bananas, oranges, grapes, and blueberries. Pour cooled sauce over fruit; cover and refrigerate for 3 to 4 hours before serving.</li>
