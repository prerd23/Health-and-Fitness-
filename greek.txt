    <section class="recipe">
      <h2>Ingredients</h2>
      <ul>
        <li>1 cup Greek yogurt</li>
        <li>2 tablespoons honey, preferably organic</li>
        <li>1/4 cup walnuts, roughly chopped</li>
        <li>A pinch of ground cinnamon (optional)</li>
        <li>Fresh fruit for garnish (optional)</li>
      </ul>

      <h2>Instructions</h2>
      <ol>
        <li>Begin by placing the Greek yogurt in a serving bowl. Use a spatula to smooth the top.</li>
        <li>Drizzle the honey evenly over the surface of the yogurt. The amount of honey can be adjusted to taste.</li>
        <li>Add the chopped walnuts, distributing them evenly across the yogurt.</li>
        <li>If desired, sprinkle a pinch of ground cinnamon over the top for added flavor.</li>
        <li>As an optional step, garnish with fresh fruit such as berries, sliced bananas, or apple slices.</li>
        <li>For best results, serve immediately or chill in the refrigerator for 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.</li>
        <li>Enjoy this healthy and delicious treat as a breakfast, dessert, or snack!</li>
      </ol>
